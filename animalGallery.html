Lesser Tenrec
Species: Echinops telfairi
Class: Mammal
Favourite food: Meal worms

Fun fact: There are nearly 30 different species of Tenrec, some of which have adapted to life in the trees. Other species have adapted to life on the ground, whilst others even live in water.

Animal Profiles

Latte and Mocha

Guinea Pigs
Species: Cavia porcellus
Class: Mammals
Favourite food: Romaine lettuce and hay

Fun fact: Unlike humans Guinea Pigs cannot sweat, this means that they are at risk of heat stroke if they are left out in the sun or become too hot. They do love to sit in the shade whenever possible.

Animal Profiles

Woody & Allen

Indian Stick Insects
Species: Carausius morosus
Class: Insects
Favourite food: Privet

Fun fact: Once a nymph (immature insect) has moulted (shed its skin) it will usually eat its shrivelled exoskeleton. One reason they do this is to recycle the protein instead of letting it go to waste.

Animal Profiles

Alto

Barn Owl
Species: Tyto alba alba
Class: Bird
Favourite food: Small rats

Fun fact: Barn owls have lop sided ears, one ear being lower than the other. This is so that they can pinpoint their prey exactly even if they maybe cannot see it because of long grass or similar.
