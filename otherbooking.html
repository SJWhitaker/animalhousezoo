Terms and Conditions

1 Animals

1.1

All our animals are handled on a regular basis, captive bred, very tame and friendly. However, if our animals are mistreated or held incorrectly by participants you must be aware that they may bite or scratch.

1.2

Due to the unpredictable nature of any animal, bites and scratches can occur.

1.3

At the beginning of each event we will go through some rules regarding the animals. Then before each animal is taken around to be held, our professional handler will show everyone how to correctly hold each animal. Please note participants will only be allowed to hold the animals if they have listened carefully to the instructions on how to handle them to ensure a safe and happy experience for both our animals and participants.

1.4

Animals will be returned to their travel carriers if they are mistreated, held incorrectly or start to show signs of stress.

1.5

Animals will be shown in a specific order for health and safety reasons.

1.6

When you enquire we will send you a list of all available animals at that time. Available animals could vary slightly due to circumstance beyond our control, however, we update our list regularly.

1.6.1

If there are any animals on the list that you don’t want to attend you must let us know 7 days prior to your event.

1.6.2

If an animal that was on the list is unwell on the day of your event then for animal health and welfare reasons it will not be able to attend your event.

1.7

We have no control over when the animals choose to defecate or urinate. Incase of an accident we carry baby wipes, kitchen towels, hand sanitizer, and poo bags on us to every event.

1.7.1

We cannot be held responsible for any stains or damage to any participants clothing or property in the event that an animal decides to defecate or urinate.

1.8 Do not feed any of the animals with your own food.

1.8.1 We (Animal House Zoo) will bring fresh water and any food needed for the animals.

2 Event Environment
2.1 No bouncy castles or other inflatables nearby (within 40 meters).
2.2 Music needs to be kept to a minimum, if possible no music. We must not be positioned next to speakers.
2.3 No balloons in reach of participants, decorative balloons are ok but none in reach of children incase they get popped.
2.4 If there are other activities to keep the children entertained e.g. toys/games etc. these must not be brought near the animals at any time.
2.5 Venue should be indoors unless you have stated to us that you request it outdoors, the venue will only be outdoors if the weather is suitable and the animals can be heated correctly.
2.5.1 To be heated correctly we will require either electric for heat mats to be plugged into or boiling water to refill hot water bottles.
2.6 If you hire a venue it is your responsibility to make sure that they allow animals on the premises. If we arrive and are denied access to the venue, you (the client) will still be responsible for the full cost of the booking.
3 Parking
3.1 Parking will be required on the drive if possible so we can safely unload our animals. For events where a drive isn’t available we will need to be parked as close to the doors as possible. At galas/fun days and similar events our vehicle must be able to park adjacent to our gazebo.
3.2 Free parking must be available. If there is no free parking available then you (the client) will be responsible for the parking fee.
3.3 If there is nowhere to park we might not be able to carry out your booking, in this case you (the client) will still be responsible for the full cost of the booking. Therefore we advise that you reserve us a space by the entrance.
3.4 If we are late starting the event due to parking issues we still have to finish the event at the end of your allocated time slot, as there might be other bookings for that day. You (the client) will still be responsible for the full cost of the booking. Please make sure we have somewhere suitable to park.
4 Participants
4.1 (Parties)
4.1.1 All participants must be over 3 years old.
4.1.2 Any child younger than 3 years old must have an adult over the age of 16 accompanying them at ALL times.
4.1.3 There must be 1 responsible adult present for the duration of the party for every 6 children.
4.1.4 Adults must control the children and keep them quiet and seated. If the children are too loud and not seated we will put the animals away until the children are back in their seats and quiet. Please understand this is for the safety of both our animals and the children.
4.2 (Schools)
4.2.1 A teacher MUST be present at all times to control the class and keep the children seated and quiet.
4.3 (All participants)
4.3.1 Participants MUST NOT torment other participants about animals they may be afraid of.
4.3.2 All participants MUST wash their hands BEFORE and AFTER handling the animals. We will provide hand sanitizer gel for during the event but after the event has finished it’s important that you wash your hands with warm soapy water.
4.3.3 We request that if there is food at the event that you wait until after the animals have been shown and are back in their travel carriers until the food is brought out.
4.3.3.1 Participants MUST NOT eat during the event. If someone does eat it is very important they wash their hands before handling any more animals. If your hands smell of food the animals might want a nibble so please keep your hands clean.
4.3.4 All participants MUST remain seated unless a professional handler asks you to stand for certain animals.
4.3.5 Whilst holding any animal you MUST stay with the professional handler and NOT walk away.
4.3.6 When the animals are in their travel carriers we ask that nobody taps on the travel carriers or pokes their fingers through the gaps.
4.3.6.1 If someone does put any part of their body next to or inside the travel carriers without permission we cannot be held responsible for any injuries incurred.
4.3.6.2 Please do not pick up any travel carriers unless you have asked and been granted permission by a professional handler to do so.
4.3.7 Participants must take it in turn one at a time to pet or hold each animal. If everyone tries to interact with an animal at the same time this can cause stress and discomfort to the animal.
4.3.8 All participants will have the opportunity to pet or hold each animal unless a member of staff says otherwise. We will return animals to their travel carriers if they start to show signs of stress, please understand this is for the animal’s welfare and the safety of the participants.
4.3.9 We reserve the right to end any encounter at any time if we see behaviour from participants that could put a member of staff or an animal in danger. Any action that affects the welfare of our animals will not be tolerated. Please note that if this happens then you (the client) will still be responsible for the full cost of the booking.
5 Allocated time slot
5.1 When a booking is made you are paying for the allocated time slot and not the amount of time.
5.2 For parties and other smaller events we will arrive approximately 15 minutes prior to the event starting in order to unload the animals into the venue.
5.3 For schools or other larger events that require a display setting up we will arrive at least 40 minutes prior to the event starting.
5.4 Please ensure that all the participants are ready to start at the beginning of the time slot.
5.5 If we do have to start late due to late arrivers we cannot over run the session past the allocated time slot.
5.6 So we can start on time we request that all participants have washed their hands before we begin. If participants have not washed their hands they will have to do so at the beginning of the session, therefore resulting in less time to interact with the animals.
5.7 In the unlikely event that we (Animal House Zoo) are late arriving to your event we will make sure that you get the full amount of interaction time with the animals that you booked for.
6 Payment Terms
6.1 We prefer payments to be sent via bank transfer (Bacs).
6.2 A non-refundable deposit of 30% will be required to secure your booking with the outstanding payment due 5 days before the event.
6.2.1 If you are unable to make a bank transfer please contact us so that we can discuss other payment options.
6.3 We understand that a cheque is often the easiest form of payment for certain events and we will gladly accommodate this. We do require however that you arrange for the cheque to have cleared in time for your event.
6.3.1 If on the day of your event we have not received the full amount agreed then we reserve the right to not attend your event.
6.4 If it has been agreed with us (Animal House Zoo) that you can pay cash on the day of the event for the outstanding balance then we MUST receive payment before we start the event.
6.4.1 If we do not receive payment we reserve the right to leave. Or we can wait until payment is received and then carry on with your event. Please note that if we wait for payment we still have to finish at the arranged time and we cannot over run. To avoid missing out on animal encounter time please make sure payment is prearranged.
6.5 Receipts can be given on request.
7 Cancellations and Refunds
7.1 Once the event is booked no cancellation will be effective until we (Animal House Zoo) have received a written cancellation by email to hello@animalhousezoo.co.uk by the person who paid the deposit.
7.1.1 To confirm that the email has been received and your booking has been cancelled, Animal House Zoo will send you a receipt of email.
7.1.2 If you do not receive a receipt of email then contact us by telephone on 07491 774424 to confirm cancellation.
7.2 All deposits are non-refundable.
7.3 If Animal House Zoo has to cancel due to unforeseen circumstances then we will re-arrange the booking to an alternative date.
7.3.1 If an alternative date is not suitable then we will offer a full refund.

Force majeure events include: Acts of God (including fire, flood, earthquake, storm, hurricane or other natural disaster), traffic accidents, threats and/or acts of terrorism, crime, war, civil war, riot, strike, interruption or failure of electricity or telephone service, or ill health of person or animals.
8.2 No party is entitled to terminate this agreement under clause 7 (Cancellations and Refunds) in a force majeure event.
