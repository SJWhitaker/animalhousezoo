Birthday Parties

Description

We provide a fun and educational Birthday party for children and adults. During the party each animal in turn will be exhibited by one of our professional handlers at which point they will also give a little speech with details about the animal. Then the partygoers will have the opportunity to hold or pet each animal.

Click here to choose from downloaded party invitation styles

For safety reasons some animals cannot be held by the public but can still be petted.
