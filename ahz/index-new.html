A Few Words About Us

Here at Animal House Zoo, our aim is to provide people of all ages with fun, interesting and educational information about a wide variety of animals including mammals, reptiles, insects, birds and gastropods.

Based in Chesterfield, Derbyshire, we are in a central location to serve the local and wider surrounding areas. We accomodate for schools, parties, care homes, fun days and more.
