About

Hi, my Name is Rachel and I am the founder and owner of Animal House Zoo. It has always been a lifelong passion of mine to work with animals. At the age of 19 I visited Australia for 6 months to work on a wildlife park. One of my duties was to present animal talks to the public for a range of species and it was at this point I decided to make this my career. When I returned from Australia I studied Animal Health and Welfare where I obtained my Foundation Degree. I then went on to study Animal and Wildlife Management where I was awarded my Bachelor of Science Honours Degree. After volunteering for a number of animal charities and companies I felt it was the right time to establish my own business doing something that I’m passionate about. So here I am, providing animal encounters in a safe and professional manner, educating our future generations on the welfare and conservation of our animals, and housing animals in accordance with the Five Freedoms listed on the Animal Welfare Act 2007.
